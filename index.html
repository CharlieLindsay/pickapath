Welcome to my pick-a-path game!

This is a simple game I have made as part of Stardance to try and improve my html, css, and JavaScript skills. Although the stories are short, and I'm not too skilled with storytelling, I hope you enjoy this simple site! Each part of the story comes with 2 buttons (unless you are finished or fail) and you need to click on the one you want to do to continue!
