Pick-A-Path

Level One

Further in we go! Ahead of us there is a man standing guard next to the left path. "I would not recommend going that way," he says. He maintains eye contact with you. "There is a landslide on the left path. The right way is safe as far as I know," he grins. Do you trust him?

The ground starts sloping slightly. That must have been a lie the man told, you think. Just to keep me away from something good. That's why he grinned at the end! You remember the way the man had maintained eye contact, and how unnerving it was to think that the man could lie that well. Do you keep going left or go through some trees to the right path?

You take the right path, and before long, realise that the man was telling the truth. Nice! He maintained eye contact, which is difficult for those who lie. That was a close call! The woods end here. You can go back to the start, or go on towards the port.

NOOOO! The land suddenly starts feeling unsettled Suddenly you find yourself falling. You failed.
