Level One

Ohh! Into the woods we go! You enter the tree canopy, branches criss-crossing overhead. You have almost begun to wonder if this is even much of an adventure or just a peaceful walk in the forest when suddenly... there's a scraping sound to your left. Do you go left to check it out. Or go away to the right?

You realise that the sound is coming from an infamous beast, known for its ferocity in your town. No one has ever survived being near it before. You can go left in the direction of the beast or right and escape?

Smart person! You start to walk away. You live on to fight another day! Ahead is another crossroad. One leads left further into the woods and the other goes right towards the port.

NOOOO! Are you crazy?! You failed.
