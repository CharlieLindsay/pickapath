Pick-A-Path

Level One

You run towards the boat! The sound of pounding footsteps behind you charge you to run even faster! While jumping aboard, you look back to see the figures even closer. One of them is grinning. A chill runs down your spine.

Looking around, you realise the boat you find yourself on is a simple one level yacht with an outboard motor. You run to the controls, which are still on. Pressing down on the throttle, the boat starts to pull away. Suddenly, the boat lurches to a stop. It is still tied to the shore! You have to options. Go right to try and remove the rope in time and risk not making it before the pursuers reach the boat, or go left and jump to the next boat over and hope that it is on. Quickly, you don't have much time to decide!

You jump onto the next boat! Searching for a key, you don't find it! The persuers reach the boat and jump aboard. Suddenly, the leaders smile doesn't seem so malicious. "Well, this is one way to introduce myself to my child," he chuckles. "You made quite the run for it!"
Your eyes open. Could this be true? Was your father who you had never met before standing right in front of you?
This is the moment you really need to decide, it could change your life. You inch forwards, you have a clear view to unattach the boat with the running engine, and quickly step across, and you would be away. Will you go left on a new life and trust this man is your long lost father, or right and escape?

You leap forwards to reach the rope. Yes! You unattach the boat in time. It lurches away from the dock. Frustrated cries ring out from the dock. Nice job! You made it! You can now exit the level, or beach the boat somewhere and go into the woods to continue adventuring? Click here to find out what would have happened if you had stayed.
