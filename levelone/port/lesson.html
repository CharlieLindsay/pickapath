Pick-A-Path

Level One

You came here for answers, and answers shall come.
If you had not launched the boat, the men would have reached you, but not attacked.
One of them would introduce themselves as your father. If you choose to trust them, you would be correct, and live the rest of your life with a father, who went to great lengths, including lighting a fire to attract his child, to find you.
This wasn't supposed to have a moral, but now that I'm here, I want to point out that you never know what could happen, and you never can judge a book by it's cover. One path acts as though the player is victorious, because they are none the wiser, but the other path and they would know.

But remember!! I am not saying to trust random men who have been lighting fires and run up to you should be safe, and to trust them if they say they are your father!! It's just a metaphor.
