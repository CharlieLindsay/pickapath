Pick-A-Path

Level One

Awesome! You walk towards the port. To your left there is some large containers lined up along the shore. One of them is open with smoke emitting. When you walk closer you realise it is a fire, someone is inside stoking it! As you watch, the fire begins to spread. Do you choose left to advance to help prevent it? You could also go right to the nearby payphone to call the police?

Getting closer, you realise there is a whole group of people, laughing to themselves, and running around the fire. You feel slightly intimidated. You have courage. You could prevent disaster! Everyone would know of you as a hero. Do you continue left towards them? It's not too late to turn right to the payphone and call the police!

Phew! I was worried you would go over to them! You go over to the payphone. The emergency services answer the phone, and you explain what is happening. "Don't worry. We will send out some officers now. Stay where you are, don't leave the port," they say. Those final words seem slightly strange. Then you realise, the voices have gone quiet! Too late you notice the cable running from the phone to the container. It was a plant to catch people attempting to call the police!

Figures begin to emerge from the container. Heart racing, you know you have two options. You can go left towards a small boat, and attempt to make a run for it (by hijacking the boat). You could also go right into the woods to hide. You know you will be safe in the woods, and will start exploring those, but you will never be able to get justice, and find out what is happening with these people.

NOOOO! I tried to warn you, but you have decided to approach a whole gang of people starting a fire on a port singlehandedly. It's a bit tragic, but as they begin to walk towards you, you faint and hit your head. You forget everything and now the rest of what happens is irrelevant. You lost!
