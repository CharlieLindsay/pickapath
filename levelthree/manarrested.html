Pick-A-Path

Level Three

"My boss," you say. The man glares at you for a second. He seems to discount you as a threat. Suddenly, his eyes open wide as though he remembers a schedule, and turns back to the wall. You hear the automated voice telling the passengers the next stop is approaching. When the doors open, police are standing outside.

They enter the carriage and make a beeline for the man in the corner. He snarls at you, "You better watch your back now." He grimaces as handcuffs are applied, and he is marched away.
You are suspicious of what he was doing. Do you stay aboard the train, or evacuate everyone in case?
