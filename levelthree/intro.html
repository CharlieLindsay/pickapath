Level Three

You run towards the base of the slide, screaming as you do so. "HEY, STOP!!!" you call out. "The slide is broken!"
The figure looks over the rail for a second, but then continues walking up. You continue screaming and, finally, they look down at you.

You yell at them about the slide being damaged, and they start walking back down, and use the chain at the bottom to close the slide. Phew! that was a close call! You can now rest back at the room, or go check out the captain's area.
