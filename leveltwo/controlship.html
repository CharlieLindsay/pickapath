Pick-A-Path

Level Two

You rush to the controls! You look down and realise that the throttle is a complex 4-part lever. Each lever corresponds to an engine from left to right! You turn off the two left engines, and set the right two to maximum. You then swing the wheel all the way to the left. The ship begins to swing to the left. You realise that you don't know where you are going, or if there is anything else unsafe. As you pass the iceberg closely on your right, you bring down all the engines. Soon, the cruise ship is stationary. Nice work!

You can now choose to run away to avoid getting celebrated, and relax back at your room. You could also go and run to get the medics for the captain.
