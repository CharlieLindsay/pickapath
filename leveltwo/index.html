Pick-A-Path

Level Two

Ah, welcome back! Get ready to start your second adventure! In this one you start on a cruise ship! It is a beautiful ship, with countless levels, movie cinema, pools, and more!

Nearby, there is an epic waterslide. It stretches very, very high up! Looking up, something seems off to you. A part of the slide has come off! As you watch, you see someone going up the stairs to go down the slide. If they try going down they will fall! You have to make a decision. Go left, and try get to the base of the slide to call up, or go right and try and reach the emergency stop. The emergency stop prevents the "go" signals at the top of the slide from showing. Trusting the person looks, they would be safe.

You run towards the emergency stop at full speed! Glancing up, you see the person has not made it to the top just yet. You could still try to run back, left, to the base of the slide and call out to them? Or you can continue on the right path?

You run towards the base of the slide, screaming as you do so. "HEY, STOP!!!" you call out. "The slide is broken!"
The figure looks over the rail for a second, but then continues walking up. You continue screaming and, finally, they look down at you.

You yell at them about the slide being damaged, and they start walking back down, and use the chain at the bottom to close the slide. Phew! that was a close call! You can now rest back at the room, or go check out the captain's area.

You continue running towards the emergency stop button. Why was it so far away? you think to yourself. Eventually, you reach the emergency stop button, press it hard. You look up at the person above, but they ignore the signal! They jump into the slide and begin sliding down! Sorry, You failed!
