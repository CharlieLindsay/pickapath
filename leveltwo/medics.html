Level Two

You run down the steps away from the control room, straight for the medical sector. Inside, you rush straight to the main desk, and tell the person there. They grab a first-aid kit, and run with you back to the control room. The medic tells others where they are going, so others will come to help.
Once you reach the captain, the medic kneels down next to the captain and breathes a sigh of relief. "Don't worry, the captain suffers from chronic fatigue syndrome," they say. "He will wake in his own time."

Later that day, it seems everyone on the ship has heard about how you saved their lives. You are a hero! A party is thrown in your honor. Good job today, you saved many lives!
