Pick-A-Path

Level Two

You head up to the captains area. There is a small group of people already there, having a look inside the control room. You step inside. It is an incredible view. You can see for miles out to sea. There are islands out to sea, and even closer... wait... is that an ice berg right ahead?! Your head whips around. The only person at the control panel and wheel is a man, who has fallen asleep in his chair! You call out to him, but he doesn't wake. Running over, you shake him, but he still doesn't wake. He appears to be having a medical emergency!

Looking ahead again, you see the ice berg is only about 2 minutes away. Thoughts of the Titanic fill your head as you try to think. You are about to get someone to go for help, but the group of tourists has left. You can either try to keep control of the ship, or go for help and hope you can run to get a second-in-command in time.
